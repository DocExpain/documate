Make Legal Documents Understandable

Signing a contract without fully understanding its terms can be risky. Whether it is an employment contract, a rental agreement, or a freelance service agreement, legal documents are often filled with jargon that obscures the true obligations and rights of the parties involved. DocuMate helps you cut through the confusion.

Key things to check in a contract

  • Termination Clauses: Understand exactly under what conditions the agreement can be ended and if there are any penalties.
  • Hidden Obligations: Make sure you know exactly what is expected of you to avoid breach of contract.
  • Auto-renewal: Many service contracts renew automatically unless you send a notice by a specific date.
  • Liability: Check what you are liable for if things go wrong.
